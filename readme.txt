Prevent wp super cache caching a page, post or cpt, by adding a shortcode

== Description ==

Prevent wp super cache from caching your content on specific pages and cpts simply by adding a simple shortcode.

Just add: [lh_no_cache_shortcode]  that is it. WP Super Cache won't cache the page!

= Does the shortcode have any attributes? =

No it just works.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `lh-no-cache-shortcode` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Include the shortcode: [lh_no_cache_shortcode]  in your content.
